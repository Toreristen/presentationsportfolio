jørns biler

Jørns Biler ApS is an used car dealer located near Odense who sells and buys used cars. Jørns Biler ApS assigned a team and I to make a redesign of their website.

The part i primarily stood for was the layout and design. I worked a lot with the C.R.A.P principles to make the site as sharp as possible. The goal was to make the site more user friendly and comfortable to navigate. The team and I took some inspiration from the original websites color palette and layout structure but refined and tweaked it so it was easier to navigate when visiting.
