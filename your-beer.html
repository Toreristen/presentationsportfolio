your beer

Your Beer was an newly founded webshop, whos goal is to find the right beer to their customers.

I was assigned to design their website from the ground up.
They wanted their site to be masculine and old fashioned but with a modern layout that's easy to navigate.
The color pallette has an old fashioned feel to it but the layout of the website has sharp edges that helps to give it a more modern look.
The header was designed to be a triangle to give the site a more unique appearance. So it is memorable and recognizable.
