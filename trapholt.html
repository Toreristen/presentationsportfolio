Art installation

Trapholt is a museum for modern art located in Kolding who assigned a team and I to make a new concept of their installation "your installation".

We proposed a draw and guess game concept, where the museum visitors can play at the end of their visit to give a more playful and adventurous event at Trapholt.
The game is played with an app which each player uses on their own mobile phones.
The player whos turn it is to draw, draws on a whiteboard connected to the app. Then the others uses the app on their phone to guess what has been drawn.

I was a big part of the design and layout of the app. The colors and graphical layout was inspired by Trapholts own brand identity and I used that to make the app unique for Trapholt.
