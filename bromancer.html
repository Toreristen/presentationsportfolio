bromancer

I was assigned to create a layout and design for an web app called Bromancer. Bromancer is an app designed for men and women to find new friends in their area. The apps concept is very similar to that of other datingapps and that is also true for it's design. In the app you have the opportunity to find and meet new acquaintances in either a community, an organisation or just individuals with the same motive. Then you swipe and like what or who you find interesting. I designed the app with the color green in mind, which is a color that represents hope, calm and is a more neutral color than pink or blue.
