Animation

I was assigned to make an animation for a Star Wars themed website.

They requested me to make an animation with Darth Vader who ignites his lightsaber.
The inspiration for the animation came from the movie Rogue One, where at the end Darth Vader appears in a hallway and ignites his lightsaber.

The animation was designed in Adobe Illustrator and then coded and animated in HTML5 and CSS.
